aao-dev-components

Shared UI components for All Aboard Ohio developer program sites.

<aao-site-header> — Site attribution header

Identifies a site as part of the AAO Developer Program. Carries the AAO logo, a link to the developer portal, and a link to allaboardohio.org. Dismissible — dismissal persists per-user in localStorage.

Standard — full branded bar

Full-width branded header. Use at the very top of tools and sub-sites that are part of the AAO Developer Program.

<aao-site-header></aao-site-header>

Dismissed? Open DevTools → Application → Local Storage → delete aao-dismissed-site-header-standard

Compact — thin attribution bar

Single-line bar. Use at the top of pages where the standard bar is too prominent, or at the bottom of a footer.

<aao-site-header mode="compact"></aao-site-header>

Footer context (compact)

The compact bar naturally doubles as a footer attribution strip.

<!-- last child inside your <footer> --> <aao-site-header mode="compact"></aao-site-header>

<aao-notification> — Notification bars

Announcement bars that sit below the site header or below site navigation. Can be driven by a central JSON config (flip "active": true in banner.json to broadcast to every AAO site at once) or via inline attributes.

Standard (info)

<aao-notification config-url="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/all-aboard-ohio/aao-dev-components/main/banner.json" ></aao-notification>

Standard (warning)

type="warning"

Standard (success)

type="success"

<aao-notification mode="lite" ...></aao-notification>

<aao-notification mode="dark" ...></aao-notification>
