aao-banner

All Aboard Ohio shared notification component — four display modes

compact

Government-attribution bar. Always visible, no config fetch. Designed for top-of-page or footer placement. Modelled on the USWDS site attribution pattern.

<aao-banner mode="compact"></aao-banner>
standard

Default notification bar. Warm AAO beige with icon, message, optional CTA link, and dismiss button. Reads from the central banner.json config.

<aao-banner config-url="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/all-aboard-ohio/aao-dev-components/main/banner.json" mode="standard" ></aao-banner>
lite

Low visual weight, blue tint. Good for informational notices that shouldn't compete with primary content.

<aao-banner config-url="..." mode="lite" ></aao-banner>
dark

AAO dark blue background with white text. High contrast. Use when the surrounding page is light and you want the banner to anchor the top strongly.

<aao-banner config-url="..." mode="dark" ></aao-banner>
type variants

The type field changes the icon. Works across all modes. Options: info, warning, success.

type="info" | type="warning" | type="success"
footer placement (compact)

Compact mode at the bottom of a footer — attributes the site without taking up visual space.

<!-- Inside your <footer>, after all content --> <aao-banner mode="compact"></aao-banner>
